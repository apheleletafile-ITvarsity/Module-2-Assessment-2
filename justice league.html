Justice League

Director: Zack Snyder

Release Date: Nov 16, 2020

Runtime: 2hr 1m

Cast: Ben Affleck Henry Cavill Amy Adams Gal Gadot Ezra Miller Jason Momoa Ray Fisher Jeremy Irons Diane Lane Connie Nielsen

Showing Times: 18h00 - 21h20, Fri-Sun: 08h00 - 11h30, 12h00-15h30, 16h00-19h30, 20h00-23h15;

Synopsis: Fueled by his restored faith in humanity and inspired by Superman's selfless act, Bruce Wayne enlists the help of his newfound ally, Diana Prince, to face an even greater enemy. Together, Batman and Wonder Woman work quickly to find and recruit a team of meta-humans to stand against this newly awakened threat. But despite the formation of this unprecedented league of heroes-Batman, Wonder Woman, Aquaman, Cyborg and The Flash-it may already be too late to save the planet from an assault of catastrophic proportions.
